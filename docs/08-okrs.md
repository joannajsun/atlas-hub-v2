# OKRs
**Atlas Hub 2.0 — Last-Mile Delivery Operations Platform**
*May 2026 | Joanna Sun*

---

## Framework

Quarterly cadence. Key Results are outcomes, not tasks — measurable and falsifiable. Target grade: 0.7–0.8. A 1.0 means the goal was too easy.

---

## Q3 2026 — Build and Launch

**O1: Ship a v1 product that Maria would choose over her current workflow**

| KR | Target |
|----|--------|
| 3 dispatch leads complete a full week of exception logging without falling back to spreadsheets | 3 users |
| Morning Brief loads in < 2s at p95 | p95 < 2s |
| Exception logging median time < 60 seconds | Median < 60s |
| 0 critical bugs (data loss, incorrect FACR, broken notifications) at launch | 0 |

**O2: Sign the first 5 paying customers**

| KR | Target |
|----|--------|
| 5 signed contracts at ≥ $1,000/month by end of Q3 | 5 contracts |
| All 5 onboarded and logging exceptions within 14 days of contract signature | 100% ≤ 14 days |
| NPS ≥ 50 from dispatch users at Day 30 | NPS ≥ 50 |

---

## Q4 2026 — Prove Impact, Grow to 20 Customers

**O1: Demonstrate measurable FACR improvement**

| KR | Target |
|----|--------|
| Customers using Atlas 90+ days show ≥ 5pp FACR improvement vs pre-Atlas baseline | ≥ 5pp |
| Exception recurrence rate (same address + reason within 30 days) down 20% | -20% |
| 2 customers can point to a specific pattern Atlas surfaced that they acted on | 2 customers |

**O2: Grow to 20 customers, $240K ARR**

| KR | Target |
|----|--------|
| 20 active paying customers | 20 |
| ARR ≥ $240K | $240K |
| Monthly churn ≤ 2% | ≤ 2%/mo |

---

## Notes

FACR is the North Star but I'm not using it as a Q3 KR — we don't have customer baselines yet and can't credibly claim credit for movement in the first few weeks of use. Q3 KRs are proxy metrics (adoption, behavior, time-to-log). FACR as a direct KR starts Q4 with 90-day cohort data.
