## TAM

Global last-mile delivery software was ~$3.5B in 2023, projected to reach $8.6B by 2028 (CAGR ~22%). Drivers: e-commerce volume growth, rising consumer expectations, labor cost pressure. US e-commerce hit $1.1T in 2023; big-and-bulky online sales grew 34% since 2021.

*Sources: Grand View Research, Allied Market Research, US Census Bureau*

---

## SAM

US white-glove delivery market is ~$15B in total delivery revenue. Approximately 2,800 regional carriers operate in this space (furniture, appliances, fitness equipment). Typical tech spend: $800–$2,500/month across TMS + telematics + route tools.

Assuming 40% of carriers are in the digitization window (not using enterprise TMS, open to modern tooling): ~1,120 addressable carriers. At $15K average ACV, that's ~$16.8M ARR at full penetration — adjusting for realistic market share gives **SAM: ~$420M** (full software stack, not just Atlas's portion).
